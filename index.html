About GDG Gwalior


Google Developer Group Gwalior is inspired by GTUG/GDG Family . We started our journey in November 2019. We try to engage student developers, fresh graduates and professionals through our hack events & meetups. The motive is to create a local ecosystem of programmers & hackers in and around Gwalior. The technology awareness is main goal for first few years of the group.
